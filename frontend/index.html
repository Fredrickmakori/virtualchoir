How It Works

Use one simple story in every demo and sales call.

This is the clearest explanation of the product: upload a score, generate practice audio, collect singer takes.

1

Upload MusicXML or PDF

Bring in the score directors already have. MusicXML goes straight in; PDF is converted first when Audiveris is available.

2

Generate rehearsal audio

Render note data into MIDI and WAV so each section has something usable between rehearsals.

3

Collect section takes

Ask singers to upload or record takes by voice part and keep the feedback loop attached to the same score workflow.
