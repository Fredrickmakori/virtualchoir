Why Split It

The multi-window design improves both product use and sales demos.

Less cognitive overload

Directors land in a focused screen instead of a single page that mixes practice, studio, and pilot tasks together.

Cleaner demo narrative

You can now say: "This is the home page. Here is the practice workspace. Here is the singer studio. Here is the pilot desk."

Better product growth

The new route structure gives you room for future additions like conductor dashboards, score libraries, and ensemble settings.
